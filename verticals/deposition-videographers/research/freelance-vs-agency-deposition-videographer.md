    14→### 3. Industry Standards and Best Practices
    15→- **Freelancers**: Direct communication, personalized attention, portfolio review essential; ideal for small/single projects; one project at a time for focus.[4][5]
    16→- **Agencies**: Team-based (editors, designers, strategists, project managers); quality control, defined workflows, full-service (thumbnails, strategy); in-house equipment standard.[1][2][4][7]
    17→- **Best practices**: Assess project complexity/deadlines—use agencies for high-stakes (e.g., fixed deadlines, technical content); freelancers for budget-limited one-offs; evaluate portfolios and past work.[3][4]
    18→- **Decision aid (5 questions)**: Fixed deadline? Complex topic? Multiple formats/languages? High visibility? Team scalability needed? Yes → agency.[3]
