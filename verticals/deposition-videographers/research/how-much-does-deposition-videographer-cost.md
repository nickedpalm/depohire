    32→### 4. Common Pain Points and Solutions
    33→- **High total costs from multi-pro add-ons**: Videographer + reporter ($100–$200/hr) + attorneys ($200–$600/hr) = $1,000–$5,000[2]. *Solution*: Opt for remote to cut travel/venue[5][7].
    34→- **Extras inflate bills**: Editing, expedited delivery, custom formats[2][6]. *Solution*: Bundle unedited raw files; avoid unless trial-critical[1].
    35→- **Travel/availability**: Extra fees in remote areas[4]. *Solution*: Nationwide remote services (e.g., Zoom)[4][8].
    36→- **Tech failures**: Audio interference, power issues[3]. *Solution*: Backup recorders, ethernet, battery/hotspot kits[3].
    37→- **Cancellation**: $99 fee for live remote[10]. *Solution*: Confirm availability early.
