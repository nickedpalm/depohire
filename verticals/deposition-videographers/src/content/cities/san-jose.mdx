## How to Choose a Deposition Videographer in San Jose

Silicon Valley litigation has specific requirements. Here's what matters:

- **CLVS Certification** — The NCRA's Certified Legal Video Specialist credential is essential. Tech litigation frequently ends up in front of judges and juries who will see your deposition video — certification ensures professional standards are met
- **IP and tech litigation experience** — San Jose depositions routinely involve software engineers, product managers, and technical experts explaining complex systems. Your videographer needs to handle screen-share captures, exhibit displays, and multi-device setups without fumbling
- **Northern District of California familiarity** — The San Jose division of the Northern District handles a massive patent and trade secret docket. Videographers who regularly work these cases understand the procedural expectations and judge-specific preferences
- **South Bay and Peninsula coverage** — Depositions happen at law offices and corporate campuses from downtown San Jose to Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, and Palo Alto. Confirm coverage without per-mile surcharges across Santa Clara County
- **Secure, encrypted streaming** — Tech companies involved in litigation often require end-to-end encrypted streaming for depositions involving proprietary code, trade secrets, or pre-release products

> **Pro Tip:** Many San Jose depositions happen at corporate campuses (Apple, Google, Cisco, Adobe) with strict visitor policies. Your videographer needs to know about badge requirements, equipment screening, and restricted photography areas. Send them the campus visitor rules in advance — showing up unprepared at a tech company's security desk wastes everyone's time.
